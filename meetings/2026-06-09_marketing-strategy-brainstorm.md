# Meeting: Lean 2-Week Growth Strategy (4-agent brainstorm)
Date: 2026-06-09

## What we discussed
Orchestrated a brainstorm with 4 specialist agents (Product Manager, Growth
Hacker, Outbound Strategist, Content Creator) to design a lean marketing strategy
for rapid growth in traffic, master registrations, and sharing — ≤€100, ~1–2
hrs/day, cold start (no owned audience), one flagship city (Milan), 2-week window.
Founder KPIs: 100 organic master registrations, 1,000 weekly visitors, 2–3 free
publications. Full strategy: `docs/marketing/2026-06-09_lean-growth-sprint.md`.

## Options considered
- **Net-new onboarding push** (founder's prior priority) — pro: builds the
  self-submit flow that was prioritized; con: cold supply acquisition is the
  hardest marketplace motion, ~unwinnable at 100 in 14 days with no audience.
- **Finish the paused claim flow** — pro: 321 scraped cards already exist; claiming
  is a 2-tap, auto-approved, warm "registration"; ~80% built; feeds a self-running
  share loop. Con: reverses the earlier onboarding-first call.
- **Channel: Telegram vs IG/TikTok** — Telegram won unanimously (native to product,
  one-tap in-app register/claim, forward mechanic = free distribution).
- **PR: traditional media vs diaspora community channels** — community pins/bloggers
  win; real journalism unrealistic in 14 days.
- **Milan vs Rome** — Milan held (denser working-age diaspora + active service economy + livelier chats).

## Decisions made
- **Redefine KPI #1 as "activations" (claims + submits), not net-new self-submits.**
  Honest targets: 40–80 activations, 600–1,200 visitors, 3–5 community features.
- **The claim → verified card → share loop is the core engine** (breaks
  founder-outreach dependence; exploits existing OG-image infra).
- **Days 1–3 = ship the engine** (mount claim flow, logged-out card CTA, one-tap
  share, verified badge, funnel instrumentation) before any outreach.
- **Distribution = Telegram channel + admin-partnered seeding** (DM admins, hand
  ready-to-pin posts, answer recurring "recommend a master" questions — never cold link-drop).
- **Day-4 kill/scale gate**: claim-start→success rate (≥30%) and organic vs
  founder-DM claim mix.
- Don't do: paid ads, SEO-as-2-week-traffic, Italian press, Milan+Rome split, cold link-dropping.
